    • About Us

    Industrial Divers Corporation (IDC) is located in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. We are a commercial diving company that has been in business since 1984. We provide diving services in inland, coastal, and offshore waters. IDC is staffed with seventeen fulltime qualified commercial divers, and maintains a very extensive inventory of state of the art diving equipment.

    Capabilities & Equipment

    Our Capabilities Include:

    • Biological and Cultural Resource Surveys and Documentation
    • Cable and Pipeline Lay, Installation, Locating, Tracking, Stabilization and Burial
    • Coral Repair, Relocation, and Transplantation
    • Cable and Pipeline Lay, Installation, Locating, Tracking, Stabilization and Burial
    • Foundation and Cofferdam Specialist
    • Installation and Maintenance or Marine Moorings, Embedment Anchors, and Regulatory Buoys
    • Florida Power & Light Plant Maintenance and Construction
    • Propeller & Shaft Changes while vessel is water born
    • Underwater Pipe Penetration Specialist
    • Underwater Welding & Cutting
    • Underwater Hull Cleaning and Repairs
    • United States Coast Guard Aids to Navigation Underwater Environmental Surveys and Wreckage Removal
    • Wastewater Plant Inspection and Repairs
