    • Dredging Services

    IDC provides diving services to several water districts in South Florida. Services include visual inspections level I & II with or without CCTV of storm water culverts. Removal of sediment from culverts pipe and structures with diver operated dredging equipment. IDC is also experienced in assisting construction companies in the installation of storm water culverts and structures, water lines, & sewer lines.
