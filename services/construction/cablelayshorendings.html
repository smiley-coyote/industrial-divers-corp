    Cable Lay, Shore Endings, & Sensor Range Installation

    IDC continues to be involved in cable installation projects for the last 25 years. IDC has been continuously providing for all aspects of submarine cable installation, shore endings, and sensor ranges for the United States Naval Research Community. Services include but are not limited to:

    • Sensor range array refurbishments, routine maintenance, surveys, installation, etc
    • Mooring/marker buoy inspections, recoveries, and installations
    • Cable landings, surveys, repairs and stabilization
    • Directional drilling support
    • Acoustic testing support
    • Under water cable tracking and marking
