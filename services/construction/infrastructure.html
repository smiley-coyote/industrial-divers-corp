    Infrastructure

    IDC can perform NDT Level 1,2 & other testing on seawalls, bridge foundations, and any other structure in and around the water. CCTV can monitor the divers work so topside can follow along with the inspection in real time.

    IDC has extensive experience inspecting, cleaning, and repairing culverts pipes of all different sizes down to 24”, we have an ROV (remotely operated vehicle) to inspect pipes smaller than 24” in diameter. We have contracts with several clients to inspect their culvert system annually looking for debris buildup in the pipe or damage to the pipe itself. In addition we do:

    • Locating and tracking under water utilities for construction and dredging projects
    • Sewer/water line installation and demolition
    • Pump station maintenance/demolition
    • Bridge piling inspection, NDT testing and repairs
    • Seawall inspection, NDT testing and repairs
    • Repair drainage culverts, water pipes, sewage pipes
